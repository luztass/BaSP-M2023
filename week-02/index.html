About us:

Megarocket SA gym was founded in 1995 by a former Olympic athlete named Max Strong. Max had always been passionate about fitness and wanted to create a gym that would provide people with a welcoming and inclusive environment to pursue their fitness goals. Over the years, Megarocket SA grew into one of the most popular gyms in the city, with a thriving community of members who were passionate about fitness. Max continued to run the gym until he retired in 2020.

Out thoughts

At Megarocket SA, we believe that fitness is not just a hobby, it's a lifelong journey. Our expert trainers are committed to helping you achieve your goals. We believe that fitness is not a one-size-fits-all solution. That's why we offer a wide range of classes and services to cater to every individual's unique needs and preferences.
